About Our Community

Way West Apartments provides quality, affordable housing options for individuals and families. Our gated community is designed to create a secure environment where residents can thrive.

We offer income-based rental rates and participate in various housing assistance programs to ensure that comfortable, safe housing is accessible to those who need it most.

Our on-site management team is dedicated to maintaining a clean, safe environment and responding promptly to resident needs.

Leasing Process

Our apartments get leased out very fast. To apply:

  • Submit your application through Zillow
  • Provide income stubs showing your last 3 months of salary
  • Income requirements vary by household size
  • Additional documentation may be required during the application process

We work closely with applicants to ensure they are able to successfully lease an apartment. Fill out our contact form to be added to our notification list for upcoming availabilities.
